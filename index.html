Master of Business Administrationg

PPM is designed for graduates who want to move into project management, either in their current technical and business field, or into a new field of expertise.

This course will provide students with a broad set of skills which have wide application. During the course of the year, they’ll gain practical experience through applied project activities and current case studies, delivered by professionals in the field.

College of Health and Life Sciences

The Masters in Public Health program is aimed towards preparing students from a wide range of professional disciplines to become leaders in public health. Population health is multidisciplinary and requires a coordinated community effort.

ATU’s public health program ins underpinned by a realization that the attainment of good health is a right not a privilege and to attain the goal of Health for All in the community leaders and policy makers must have a sound grounding on all the issues that impact on their communities health and well-being.
